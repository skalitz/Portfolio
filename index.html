Rates & Contact Info

If you're in need of a proofreader, copy editor, or content manager, I'd love to hear from you!

My general rate for proofreading (spell checking, grammar, style) is $30/hour; copy editing (coherence, structure, arcs) is $34/hour. I can also provide a flat-rate for larger projects. However, I know that no project is the same and am happy to discuss specific needs beforehand so that we can arrive at an appropriate rate.

Email me with a brief overview of your project and any questions you may have and we'll set up a time to chat.
